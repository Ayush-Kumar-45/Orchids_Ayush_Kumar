Le Chatelier's Principle

When a system at equilibrium is subjected to change in concentration, temperature, volume, or pressure, the system adjusts itself to counteract the effect of the applied change and a new equilibrium is established.

2A(g) + B(g) ⇌ C(g) + Heat
